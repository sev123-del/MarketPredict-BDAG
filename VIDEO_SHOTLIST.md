# Video Shotlist & Narration — MarketPredict (BDAG)

Target length: 90–150 seconds

00:00–00:08 — Intro (voiceover + title)
- Shot: Project title card "MarketPredict (BDAG)" + 2–3 second logo
- Narration: "MarketPredict — secure, low-fee prediction markets on BlockDAG."

00:09–00:25 — Problem and solution (voiceover)
- Shot: Brief text overlay describing problem (fees, UX complexity)
- Narration: "Many prediction markets are expensive and hard for new users. We built MarketPredict to simplify participation with low fees and clear protections."

00:26–00:55 — Live demo core flow (connect, deposit, create, predict)
- Shot: Open demo site, connect wallet, deposit into dApp balance, create market (fill form), show live odds preview.
- Narration: Explain each step concisely and highlight the payout preview.

00:56–01:10 — Multi-user flow and odds update
- Shot: Show two wallets or Play Money mode making opposing predictions; show pool/odds updating in real time.
- Narration: "Odds update instantly and payouts follow the parimutuel model."

01:11–01:25 — Resolution & claim
- Shot: Resolve market (admin or test helper), show claiming winnings flow, show transaction on BlockDAG explorer.
- Narration: "After resolution, winners claim their payouts; fees are taken only from profits."

01:26–01:40 — Security features & UX
- Shot: Open settings, show spending limit, enable self-restriction, show prevented action.
- Narration: "Built-in protections include spending limits and self-restriction to encourage responsible participation."

01:41–01:50 — Technical/closing
- Shot: Show `README.md` in repo, contract address and explorer verification.
- Narration: "Source code and contract are on GitHub; live demo and contract address are in the README."

01:51–End — Call to action
- Shot: Project URL and contact info overlay.
- Narration: "Try the demo or check the repo. Thank you."

Recording tips
- Use a single monitor; capture 1080p at 30fps.
- Keep mouse movements deliberate and avoid long pauses.
- Record narration separately and sync, or record live voiceover.

Suggested audio script (short)
- "MarketPredict — secure, low-fee prediction markets on BlockDAG."
- "We make prediction markets simple and affordable, with Play Money for onboarding and real BDAG mode for serious users."
- "Create a market, place a prediction, see odds update in real time, and claim winnings after resolution."
- "Security features include spending limits, self-restriction, and a 48-hour resolution delay to protect users."

# Demo Instructions — MarketPredict (BDAG)

Goal: Provide a short, reproducible walkthrough testers can run easily.

Prerequisites
- BDAG-compatible wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Testnet BDAG (faucet) for Chain ID 1043

Walkthrough steps (quick, 90–120s demo)
1. Open the live demo: https://market-predict-bdag-cr2n.vercel.app/
2. On homepage, at bottom click button "Fund Your Account."
3. Connect wallet and switch to BlockDAG Awakening Testnet (Chain ID: 1043).
4. Deposit several test BDAG into the dApp balance.
5. Click "MarketPredict" logo in top left corner of screen.
6. Click "Explore All Markets" toward the bottom of the screen.
7. Click on 1 of the 3 Market Questions.
8. Click YES or NO.
9. Click the textbox and enter an amount of 1 or more. Or you may click one of the prefilled amounts without requiring typing.
10. Click Submit below the textbox.

# Demo Instructions — MarketPredict (BDAG)

Goal: Provide a short, reproducible walkthrough testers can run easily.

Prerequisites
- BDAG-compatible wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Testnet BDAG (faucet) for Chain ID 1043

Walkthrough steps (quick, 90–120s demo)
1. Open the live demo: https://market-predict-bdag-cr2n.vercel.app/
2. Go to **Wallet**.
3. Connect your wallet and switch to BlockDAG Awakening Testnet (Chain ID: 1043).
4. Deposit several test BDAG into your MarketPredict (in-app) balance.
5. Go to **Markets** and open any active market.
6. Pick **YES** or **NO**.
7. Under **Amount to Predict**, either type an amount or tap a preset (1 / 10 / 100 / 1K / 10K) or **Max**.
8. Click **Submit**.

Optional (nice-to-show)
- Open **Settings** → set your preferred **Timezone** (affects the displayed close time).
